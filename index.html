Accomodation

La Baraka is a four bedroom bungalow (holiday house) located in Flic en Flac, one of the most beautiful beaches in Mauritius. It is a mere 300 metres from the public beach with easy access to a supermarket and a wide range of restaurants and shops. There is easy access to public transport.

Amenities

4 fully furnished bedrooms, 1 bathroom and 1 separate toilet, kitchen, lounge, dining area, veranda. 1 undercover garage and space for additional cars.

  • Swimming pool (includes a kids area)
  • Air conditioning
  • 24 hours Gatekeeper
  • Satellite TV
  • Charcoal BBQ

Housekeeping and home cooked Mauritian cuisine meals available (at additional costs)
